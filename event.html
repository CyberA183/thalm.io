PURPOSE

The purpose of the THALM Art of Work show is to expose the general ausience to not only famous but upcoming Sims 4 content creators. Apart from the initial fashion show, the event will include a variety of workshops for those interested in creating content and Q&As to learn more about the various creators.

Due to the renewed spread of Covid-19, the team at THALM concluded that it would be safer to have the show virtually, instead of displayed to a large, physical audience. Each creator will still have the speak during an alloted period of time and questions can be asked during either the workshops or the Q&As. Please reserve for the event down below so that we can save your spot for the workshops.
