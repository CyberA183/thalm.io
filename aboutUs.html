ABOUT US

THALM is a fashion distribution design brand founded by Ariel Thompson, Julia Tripp, and Zoe Tripp. The aim of the brand is to push Sims 4 content creators who design and produce clothing through Maxis Match. Popular creators in the Sims community such as NolanSims and AHarris00Britney grace the site with much upcoming and old content.

At first Thalm was initially designed to be its own fashion line but due to time constraints and a steep learning curve, we didn't have the ability to accomplish what we wanted. After going back through some of our ideas, the team ultimately decided that instead of creating our own fashion line, we would instead bring exposure to others. Specifically exposure to creators in the Sims 4, which Ariel suggested. Through careful planning and hard work, we were able to successfully build a website that accomplished our goal. Although its not the best, we later hope to improve ourselves. The team behind Thalm endlessly thank the people who have supported us through our endeavors and hope that you'll stay for more!

OUR MISSION

Thalm's mission is to expose people who are not familiar with the Sims community to outstanding fashion content creators, while also encouraging others to take up the craft. In the future, Thalm, as a whole, hopes to add more Sims 4 content creators to its site and even more to the upcoming show.
